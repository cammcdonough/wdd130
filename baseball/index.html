Welcome to Baseball

Baseball has long been considered America’s pastime. This site is designed for kids, teens, and anyone new to the sport who wants a simple, clear introduction to how baseball works.

On these pages, you’ll learn where the game came from, the basic rules you hear during a game, and the different positions players fill on the field.

What You’ll Learn

This site includes:

  • How baseball is played
  • Basic rules and scoring
  • Player positions and roles
